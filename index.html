About Us

Who We Are

Sasman & Sons Roofing and Ceilings is a proudly family-owned roofing company based in Cape Town, South Africa, with a heritage that stretches back to 1962. What started as a small local operation has grown into a trusted and well-established name in the roofing and construction industry. For more than six decades, we have remained committed to delivering reliable, high-quality workmanship backed by honesty, consistency, and a passion for excellence.

Our team specializes in a comprehensive range of services, including new roof installations, complete roof replacements, detailed roof repairs, waterproofing, ceiling installations and repairs, carpentry, structural alterations, and the supply and installation of precision-engineered roof trusses. We combine generations of hands-on experience with modern materials and innovative techniques to ensure every project meets strict industry standards for safety, durability, and long-term performance.

At Sasman & Sons, we understand that every roof plays a vital role in protecting a home or business. That is why we approach each project—big or small—with the same level of professionalism, care, and attention to detail. Our goal is to provide clients with dependable solutions, transparent communication, and results that stand the test of time. With a long history of satisfied customers, we strive to maintain the highest level of service and continue building roofs and relationships that last for generations.
